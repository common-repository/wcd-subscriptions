== WCD SUBSCRIPTIONS FOR WOOCOMMERCE FEATURES ==

**1) Create Product as a Subscriptions Product in a Click With Our WCD Subscriptions Plugin**

Admin can convert a simple product into a subscription product and get WooCommerce recurring payments, Also can assign a subscription label to simple products by simply checking a box.

**2) Automated Email Notifications for Subscription-Related Events Such as Sign-up, Renewal, and Cancellation**

With WooCommerce Subscriptions Plugin, users will get automated email notifications of subscription-related events such as signing up, renewing subscriptions, or canceling subscriptions.

**3) Create Subscription Products with Different Billing Intervals and Trial Periods**

WooCommerce subscriptions plugin creates subscription products with different billing intervals (e.g. monthly, quarterly, annually) and trial periods (e.g. 7 days, 14 days) allowing businesses to offer flexible and customizable subscription options to their customers. 

This will result in increased customer retention and satisfaction, as well as provide your business with a steady and predictable stream of revenue.

**4) Subscription Analytics to Help You Track the Performance of Subscriptions**

With the help of the WooCommerce Subscriptions free plugin, analytics can be incredibly useful in tracking subscription performance such as subscriber growth, subscriber churn rate, subscriber demographics, subscription revenue, subscription retention, customer engagement, and customer satisfaction.

**5) Ability to Pause or Cancel Subscriptions Both by Admin or Customer**

WCD Subscriptions plugin provides the flexibility to you and your users to easily cancel subscriptions at any time and stop all upcoming WooCommerce recurring payments.

**6) Offer Sign-up Fees or Free Trials for Subscriptions**

Admin can set the frequency and duration of free trials to entice new potential customers to sign up for your products or services by offering a free trial on subscription products.

**7) Compatibility With Popular Payment Gateways**

Our WordPress Subscriptions plugin is compatible with 

* [**WooCommerce Stripe Payment gateway**](https://wordpress.org/plugins/woocommerce-gateway-stripe/)
* [**PayPal Express Checkout**](https://www.paypal.com/mu/webapps/mpp/express-checkout)

**8) Compatibility With Other Plugins**

WCD Subscription is now compatible with [**Loco Translate**](https://wordpress.org/plugins/loco-translate/), which helps users generate and modify language files directly and easily within your theme or plugin.

== Installation ==

= Automatic installation =

The simplest option is automatic installation because you don't have to leave your web browser and WordPress handles the file transfers. WCD Subscriptions can be installed automatically by logging into your WordPress dashboard, selecting the Plugins menu, and clicking Add New.

Type "WCD Subscriptions" into the search box and press Search Plugins. You can review details regarding our plugin, including its date of release, rating, and description, once you've found it. Of course, the most important part is that you can install it by just clicking "Install Now."

= Manual installation =

The WCD Subscriptions plugin must be downloaded and uploaded to your website using your preferred FTP program in order to be installed manually. How to do this is explained in WordPress [here](https://wordpress.org/support/article/managing-plugins/#manual-plugin-installation).

= Updating =

Automatic updates should work flawlessly, but as usual, make sure you have a backup of your website.
